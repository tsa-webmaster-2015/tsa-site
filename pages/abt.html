ABOUT OUR CHAPTER

Our school has one of Colorado's most accomplished TSA chapters. With more members than nearly any other chapter in Colorado, a plethora of national appearances (and trophies) under our belt, and widespread support from both classmates and family members, our club is truly an integral part of our school's community. Below are just a few of the things that make our chapter special.

Our chapter has never had fewer than four top-three event finishes since its conception.

With nearly a hundred members and one of the largest state participant numbers in Colorado, our chapter is consistently a dominant force at the state competition.

Our TSA chapter sent fifteen students to the TSA national conferences last year, to compete with over 6300 others from around the world.
